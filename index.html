About Us

Stackbarks is a clever play on words that combines the idea of technology infrastructure with man's best friend - dogs. The word "stack" refers to the various layers of technology infrastructure that are built upon one another to create a stable and reliable system. "Barks" is a clear reference to the vocalization of dogs, which is often used to communicate important messages or warnings. Together, the name Stack Barks suggests a business that is both knowledgeable about IT infrastructure and able to communicate effectively with clients, much like a well-trained dog.
